Interactive Timetables for all Galway city buses

About


Galway Bus Spotter's unique interactive timetables enable users to share extra bus information.


Say you're waiting at a bus stop. When your bus arrives or if you spot another bus going by, the app lets you add this information to the relevant timetable.

It will be instantly visible to other Galway Bus Spotter users.


Say you're waiting at a bus stop again. Your bus is late and there is no real time information available.

If the bus has been spotted by anybody earlier in the route, the app will tell you where , when and how early or late it was running at the time.
